Standard Plan

The plan provides the following:
* Up to R75 000 cover for you and your spouse
* Up to R50 000 cover for up to four parents and eight children
* Up to R30 000 cover for up to eight extended family members
* The education benefit can be taken out with the Standard Funeral plan.
What is it?
* The education benefit is designed to assist with the cost of education for the policy holder's children in the event of the policy holder's death. It helps them leave a meaningful legacy.
How does it work?
* Payout on the death of main life.
* Payout is paid directly to the education institution that the child/ children attend.
* You can choose any month once a year to skip a payment, without losing your cover.
* You can choose any month once a year to skip a payment, without losing your cover.
* We'll pay you back 5 months' worth of premiums every 5 years .
* Survival benefit of 10% of premiums when child turns 18 years and principal life insured is still alive.
* On death,an Additional Expense benefit of 10% of Education benefit, for the particular year, will be paid annually to assist with items such as school uniforms, stationery and text books.
